Snow

Written by Chris Piech

Handouts: ArrayLists
Examples: Rockets

Animate GOvals (snow) falling down from the sky and pilling up on the ground.

Use an animation loop. Each time through the loop:

  1. Animate all snow flakes not on the ground
  2. Generate a new snow flake at the top of the screen with 10% probability
  3. Pause for 10 milliseconds
