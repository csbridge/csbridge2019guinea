CSBridge Guinea

Welcome to the guinean portal of the CSBridge program. The CSBride program inspires the future generation of computer scientists by teaching young people to discover the joy of programming. CSBridge is based on introductory computer science teaching methodologies that have been used at Stanford University for over 20 years. This program is held in different cities and universities around the globe, and has already been used to teach the basics of programming to thousands of high school students. This portal points you to the CSBridge sections held in Guinea. For more information on the CSBridge program, visit its official website .
