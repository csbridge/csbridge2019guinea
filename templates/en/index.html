CSBridge Sections organized in Guinea

This portal gives you access to CSBridge sections organized in Guinea. Each section has at most 10 students. Each section has an experienced section leader who lectures and supports the students individually throughout the two-week period. Section Leaders are passionate about computer science and serve as great role models to high school students.

About CSBridge

The CSBridge program inspires the next generation of computer scientists by helping young people discover the joy of programming. It is based on computer science teaching methodologies that have been used at Stanford University for over 20 years. It is is organized in different cities and universities around the world and has already been used to teach the basics of programming to thousands of high school students. For more information on the CSBridge program, visit its official website.
